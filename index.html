How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

• Bread (2 slices)

• Peanut Butter

• Jelly (your favorite flavor)

Steps

1. Take two slices of bread and lay them on a flat surface.

2. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread using a knife.

3. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread.

4. Press the two slices together, with the peanut butter and jelly sides facing each other.

5. Cut the sandwich in half (optional) and enjoy!
